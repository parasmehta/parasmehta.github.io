I am a PhD candidate at the Databases and Information Systems working group at FU Berlin. My supervisor is Prof. Agnès Voisard

Previous

Till Sept 2014, I was a member of the European project GEOCROWD.

Research

My research is in applications of user generated geospatial data in alerting and personalization of information.
