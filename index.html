Hi!

I am a PhD candidate at the Databases and Information Systems working group at FU Berlin. My supervisor is Prof. Agnès Voisard. You can email me or find me on LinkedIn.

Previous

Till Sept 2014, I was a member of the European project GEOCROWD and a recipient of the Marie Curie fellowship. Before that I had the chance to work at Consult Hyperion as Software Engineer.

I did MSc in Computer Science with the Erasmus Mundus scholarship. which was one of the best experiences in my life! I studied what I love with people from 16 different countries in three different countries- England, Spain and Greece :-). My thesis was on securing mobile commerce using XML Signature and Encryption, with Prof. Mario Muñoz Organero. My Bachelors degree is in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Delhi College of Engineering.

Research

My research is in application of user generated geospatial data for targeting and personalization of incident notifications.
